About

My Story

Hey! I’m Jessica May, a UX Researcher and Designer I began my journey in Bloomington, IN, where I got my undergraduate degree in Psychology at Indiana University. I concentrated on human behavior and motivation, and also found my passion in research. I decided to leave the Midwest to pursue something more creative in my life. I got a job organizing community events and managing a theater in Colorado. I loved working on a team and bringing people together. However, I still craved for something more creative than what I was doing in a theater. That’s when I learned about UX Design. To me, UX Design is the perfect combination of my love for psychology and research, and being able to create something useful and meaningful.
